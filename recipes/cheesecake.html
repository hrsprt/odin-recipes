Cheesecake

Image source: Wikimedia

Description

A cheesecake is a sweet dessert consisting of one or more layers. The main layer consists of a mixture of soft, fresh cheese (usually cream cheese or ricotta), eggs, and sugar. It may be baked or unbaked.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan with butter.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add sugar and vanilla, mixing well. Add eggs one at a time, mixing just until blended.
  4. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the pan.
  5. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until set. Let cool before serving.
